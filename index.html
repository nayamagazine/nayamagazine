    Mission Statement

    NAYA Literary and Arts Magazine empowers women by raising awareness about women's issues through student work.

    Student Led

    As students passionate about women's rights in modern society, we hope to inspire others to support women through the power of poetry, prose, and visual art.

    Stanford OHS

    We are a student organization at Stanford Online High School, and strive to increase social responsibility in the OHS community and beyond.

  • NAYA Team

    • Katherine Viala

      Katherine Viala is a literary and art contributor, and also assists with NAYA Magazine communications.

    • Anushka Noori

      Anushka is the founder and Editor in Chief of NAYA Magazine. She is passionate about advocating for women's rights and fostering civic engagement. Anushka has represented her state at the Scripps National Spelling Bee. Her other passions include mathematics, dance, and swimming.

    • Maryam Ali

      Maryam Ali is a NAYA Magazine literary contributor, and is passionate about gender equality. She hopes to write articles for NAYA about feminism by granting a nuanced perspective. In her free time, you'll find her reading, drawing, or researching gender, game theory, and law.
